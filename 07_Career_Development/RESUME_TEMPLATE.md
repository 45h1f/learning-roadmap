### **For Each Bullet Point:**

```
[Action Verb] + [What you did] + [Technologies used] + [Quantifiable Result/Impact]
```

### **Examples:**

‚ùå **Weak:** "Worked on the backend API"

## üé® Resume Formatting Best Practices

### **Length:**
- **0-3 years:** 1 page
- **3-10 years:** 1-2 pages
- **10+ years:** 2 pages max

### **Font:**
- Use: Arial, Calibri, Helvetica, or Garamond
- Size: 10-11pt for body, 14-16pt for name
- Avoid: Comic Sans, Times New Roman, decorative fonts

### **Spacing:**
- Margins: 0.5-1 inch on all sides
- Line spacing: 1.0-1.15
- Section spacing: Add extra space between sections

### **Colors:**
- Primary text: Black (#000000)
- Headings: Dark blue (#1a1a1a or #0066cc)
- Links: Blue (#0066cc)
- Avoid: Bright colors, gradients

### **ATS Optimization:**
- Use standard section headers (EXPERIENCE, EDUCATION)
- Avoid tables, columns, text boxes
- Use simple bullet points (‚Ä¢)
- Save as PDF (unless specifically asked for .docx)
