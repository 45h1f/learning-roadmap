- Optimization: Optimized, Improved, Enhanced, Reduced, Increased
- Problem-Solving: Resolved, Debugged, Diagnosed, Fixed

**Include Metrics Like:**
- Performance: "reduced latency by 60%", "improved load time by 3 seconds"
- Scale: "serving 100K+ users", "handling 10K requests/second"
- Business: "generated $100K ARR", "reduced costs by $40K annually"
- Quality: "achieved 99.9% uptime", "increased test coverage to 85%"
- Team: "led team of 4", "mentored 3 junior engineers"

- Organize by category for easy scanning
- Add proficiency levels for languages: Expert, Proficient, Intermediate, Familiar
- List most important/relevant skills first
- Match keywords from job descriptions

### **Certifications:**

- Include relevant online certifications (Coursera, Udemy with high-quality courses)
- Add coding platform rankings if impressive (LeetCode Top 5%, etc.)
